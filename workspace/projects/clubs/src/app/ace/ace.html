Join us

The ACE is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to fostering a passion for coding and technology. Our mission is to provide a collaborative environment where members can learn, share knowledge, and work on exciting projects together. We organize regular workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges to help members enhance their skills and stay updated with the latest industry trends. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder aiming to tackle advanced topics, the Coding Club offers something for everyone. Join us to connect with like-minded individuals, gain hands-on experience, and take your coding journey to the next level!
