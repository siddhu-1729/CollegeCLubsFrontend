Major Events That Happens

ISTE-NIPUNA

The NIPUNA event is the largest annual techno-management symposium in Andhra Pradesh, organized by the ISTE Student Chapter of SRKR. NIPUNA consistently attracts thousands of participants from various colleges and offers a vast range of technical, non-technical, and managerial competitions designed to foster skills, creativity, and leadership among engineering students.

CodingClub-HackOverFlow

Hackoverflow is a flagship hackathon organized by the SRKR Coding Club at SRKR Engineering College, specifically designed to test and enhance student programming and problem-solving skills in a competitive, collaborative setting.

CSI-TECHFLET

TechFleet is a national-level technical symposium organized annually by the Computer Society of India (CSI) Student Chapter in the Department of Information Technology. It serves as a platform where students from various colleges come together to present technical papers, participate in workshops, and showcase their skills in different technological domains.

PAIE CELL - VEDIC VISION

Vedic Vision is a hackathon event organized at SRKR Engineering College, prominently featuring coding and competitive programming challenges designed to test and enhance problem-solving abilities among students. It is one of the major tech events usually associated with the Computer Science and Engineering department or organized in collaboration with the Event Organizing Committee (EOC) and potentially the Placement Cell (PAIE Cell) to encourage learning, coding, and competition.

ACE-PRAJWALAN

"Prajwalan" is the flagship national-level hackathon organized by the Association of Computer Engineers (ACE), the student body of the Computer Science and Engineering Department at SRKR Engineering College.
This event brings together students from various colleges to collaborate, innovate, and compete in solving real-world problems through technology and coding challenges.
It is a highly anticipated coding competition and innovation event held annually, inviting students from all over India to participate.
Prajwalan 2k25, for instance, was held on February 17-18, 2025, with a prize pool of INR 50,000+.

AME-SANKETA

SANKETA is a national level student symposium organized by the Association of Mechanical Engineering Students (AME). It is a prominent event that showcases technical knowledge, innovation, and skill development specifically in the field of mechanical engineering.The symposium features various technical events, competitions, workshops, and presentations that encourage mechanical engineering students to demonstrate their expertise and creativity.

AUTOMOTIVE CLUB - GO-KART CHAMPIONSHIP

The Automotive Club rganizes the prestigious SRKR Go-Kart Championship, a national-level competition that promotes creativity, innovation, and practical engineering skills among aspiring mechanical engineers.
The event spans four days and features multiple teams competing in designing, building, and racing go-karts.
The championship attracts participation from various colleges, including women’s teams, fostering inclusivity and collaboration.
It encourages hands-on experience with automotive design, fabrication, and teamwork, aligning with AICTE's emphasis on practical education.

GDG - DEV CHALLENGE

The GDG Dev Challenge is a two-day internal hackathon organized by the Google Developer Group (GDG) on Campus team at SRKR. The event focuses on innovation, teamwork, and solving real-world problems using emerging technologies, particularly AI and Google tools. Participants have the chance to win rewards worth ₹10,000 and claim Google Cloud credits to support their projects.

AEE - SANKALP

AEE - SANKALP is the national-level technical symposium organized by the Association of Electrical Engineers (AEE) at SRKR Engineering College. It is a flagship event conducted annually by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) to showcase technical talents of both undergraduate and postgraduate students. SANKALP is known for its academic rigor and has been a regular feature in the department’s commitment to skill development and research exposure.

SWECHA - FREEDOM FEST

The SWECHA Freedom Fest is an annual festival celebrating Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). Organized by the SRKR Swecha Learning Centre in association with the college’s Technology Center, this event promotes awareness and education around open knowledge, free software principles, and people-centric technology innovations
